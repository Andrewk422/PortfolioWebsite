The Best Movies According to Andrew

My Top Movies From My Childhood (2000-2010)

1. Toy Story

I loved Buzz Lightyear - he was my favorite.

2. Finding Nemo

I thought that Dory was funny and I liked the Pelicans when they would say "mine."

3. Cars

I liked Lightning McQueen and Mater. I also liked Doc Hudson. I saw it for my kindergarten graduation present.
